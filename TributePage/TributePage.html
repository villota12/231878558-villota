The Phenom: Alyssa Valdez

She’s the picture of determination, ready to deliver a game-changing spike.

In the intense arena of the UAAP, Alyssa Valdez emerged as a shining star for the Ateneo Lady Eagles. Known for her powerful spikes and unshakeable leadership, Alyssa transformed the once-underestimated team into fierce contenders. During Season 76, the Lady Eagles, underdogs to powerhouse teams like La Salle, defied all odds. Alyssa’s relentless performance, both offensively and defensively, carried her team through a grueling series of matches, earning them a historic championship. Her composure under pressure, whether delivering a crucial point or rallying her teammates, solidified her reputation as the "Phenom."
In Season 77, Alyssa and the Lady Eagles returned even stronger. Leading with heart and skill, she steered her team to back-to-back championships, cementing Ateneo’s legacy and her place as one of the greatest UAAP volleyball players of all time. Beyond her on-court heroics, Alyssa’s humility and sportsmanship resonated with fans and fellow athletes alike. She inspired a new generation of players, showing that success comes not only from talent but from hard work, perseverance, and a passion for the game.
